Privacy Policy for Trakzoriq

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

Trakzoriq ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile Information: When you register or log in, we collect your email address, mobile number, and role (admin/operator).
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how the App is accessed and used, including tractor management logs and work assignments.
  • Device Information: We may collect platform-specific identifiers required for functionality (e.g., for PDF/Excel exports).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our Service.
  • To manage user accounts and roles.
  • To generate reports (PDF/Excel) for agricultural management.
  • To communicate with you about updates or support.

3. Data Storage and Security

We use Supabase for secure data storage. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4. Permissions

The App requires the following permissions to function:

  • Internet: To sync data with our servers.
  • Storage: To save and export PDF/Excel reports to your device.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for authentication and database management. Their privacy policy can be found on their official website.
