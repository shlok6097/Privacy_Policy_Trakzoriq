Privacy Policy for Trakzoriq

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

Trakzoriq ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

  • Profile Information: When you register or log in, we collect your email address, mobile number, and role (admin/operator).
  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how the App is accessed and used, including tractor management logs and work assignments.
  • Device Information: We may collect platform-specific identifiers required for functionality (e.g., for PDF/Excel exports).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our Service.
  • To manage user accounts and roles.
  • To generate reports (PDF/Excel) for agricultural management.
  • To communicate with you about updates or support.

3. Data Storage and Security

We use Supabase for secure data storage. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4. Permissions

The App requires the following permissions to function:

  • Internet: To sync data with our servers.
  • Storage: To save and export PDF/Excel reports to your device.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for authentication and database management. Their privacy policy can be found on their official website.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: shlok@trakzoriq.com

7. Account Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated data from Trakzoriq. We are committed to processing your request promptly and securely.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your account, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to shlok@trakzoriq.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".
  2. Include your registered email address in the email body to verify your identity.
  3. Provide any additional information if requested to confirm your request.

Upon receiving your request, we will verify your identity and proceed with the deletion process. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted within 7 days of verification.

Data Deletion

The following types of data will be deleted upon account deletion:

  • Profile information (email address, mobile number, role)
  • Login credentials
  • User activity data (tractor management logs, work assignments)

Please note that some data may be retained if required for legal, regulatory, or security purposes, such as fraud prevention or compliance with applicable laws.
